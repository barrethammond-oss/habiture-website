A LEGACY OF PLACE SINCE1971

Habiture is a lifestyle brand that celebrates nature, embraces quality, and provides design, development and management services across the globe.

Since 1971, Company Principal Travis Hammond has been active in the real estate industry around the world — from pioneering Self Storage in Washington State under the You-Store-It brand, to the construction and development of hundreds of apartments and condominiums across the Pacific Northwest. Development projects in California, Asia and Europe followed with the creation of Habiture, producing a series of exciting global opportunities.

In 2016, HF Holdings — the Hammond family private office — began investing in medical office buildings, diversified into the life-sciences sector, and is now pursuing opportunities in early-learning centers, focusing primarily in the State of Florida.
